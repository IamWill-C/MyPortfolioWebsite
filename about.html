About Me

Hey! My name is Will Clevenger and I'm a 3D artist based in the United States. I have a passion for creating immersive and visually stunning environments, props, and visual effects for video games and other interactive media. With a strong background in 3D modeling, texturing, lighting, and VFX, I strive to bring my artistic vision to life through my work. I am constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to grow as an artist and contribute to the gaming industry.

Skills
  • 3D Modeling: Blender, Maya, ZBrush
  • Texturing: Substance Painter, Photoshop
  • Lighting: Unreal Engine, Unity
  • VFX: Houdini, Niagara (Unreal Engine), Shuriken (Unity)
  • Game Engines: Unreal Engine, Unity
  • Version Control: Github
  • Task Management: Jira, Trello, Notion, ClickUp, Asana
  • Coding: HTML, CSS, Unreal Blueprints, C#
  • Other: Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects), Microsoft Office Suite

Education

Along with my Associate degree, I hold a Bachelor's degree in Digital Media for Game Design from the University of Central University, where I graduated with honors. During my time at university, I had the opportunity to work on a variety of game projects, both individually and as part of a team, which allowed me to develop my skills in 3D modeling, texturing, lighting, and VFX. As well as create long lasting friendships with like-minded individuals would also share my passion for games, I also completed several internships and freelance projects that provided me with real-world experience in the game development industry.
